### ─────────  ✦  A B O U T   M E  ✦  ─────────

</div>

I'm **Tee Tian An**, a Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics student who treats every system like a puzzle worth understanding. My mindset sits at the intersection of **defense and investigation** — I like knowing not just *how* things work, but *how they break* and *how to trace what happened*.

I move comfortably across the stack: fluent in **Python** for automation, scripting and tooling, and confident building clean, responsive interfaces with the full **front-end** toolkit. I read **C** when I need to get closer to the metal. Whatever the layer, I care about building things that are secure, thoughtful, and genuinely well-made.
